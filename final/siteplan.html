Natural Hair Care (4C Hair) Site Plan

A guide for designing and developing a natural hair care website

Site Name

Natural Crown Care

This name represents natural hair as a crown and emphasizes care, confidence, and empowerment for people with 4C natural hair.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and support individuals with 4C natural hair. It will provide routines, moisturizing techniques, protective hairstyles, product recommendations, and solutions to common hair issues such as dryness, breakage, and shrinkage.

Scenarios

  • What is the best routine for growing healthy 4C natural hair?
  • Which natural oils and products help retain moisture?
  • What protective hairstyles reduce breakage and support growth?

Color Scheme

The website uses a purple-inspired palette representing beauty, creativity, and natural care.

#6a0dad – Primary color (headings, buttons, navigation)

#9b59b6 – Secondary color (highlights and accents)

#f3e8ff – Background color (soft, light page background)

#2c2c2c – Text color (readability and contrast)

Typography

  • Roboto – Body text and general content
  • Poppins – Headings and titles

Wireframes

Mobile View

A vertical layout with stacked sections: header, hero image, introduction, hair care tips, products section, and footer.

Desktop View

A grid-based layout with side-by-side sections for tips, products, and featured content, allowing easier navigation and better visual balance.

Wireframes may be hand-drawn and submitted separately if required.
